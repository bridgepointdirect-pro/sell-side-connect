About Bridge Point

Our Mission

Bridge Point revolutionizes capacity matching in high-growth, underserved industries by connecting businesses with overflow projects to those with available capacity. We leverage AI-driven technology, rigorous verification processes, and niche-specific compliance tools to ensure optimal resource utilization and sustainable growth across five specialized sectors.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Bridge Point emerged from recognizing a critical gap in B2B matching platforms. While general AEC platforms became oversaturated, specialized niches—green construction, infrastructure, wildfire resilience, agri-labor, and tiny homes—remained underserved despite explosive growth potential. With combined expertise in construction management, technology innovation, and market analysis, we created a modular platform that adapts our proven matching algorithms to each niche's unique requirements.

Our platform addresses $500B+ in total market opportunity across industries experiencing labor shortages, regulatory complexity, and unprecedented government funding (including $1.2T from IIJA). By focusing on verification, compliance automation, and AI-powered matching, we've positioned Bridge Point as the trusted solution for businesses seeking reliable partnerships in specialized sectors.
