About Bridge Point

Our Mission

At Bridge Point, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the Architecture, Engineering, and Construction (AEC) industry by connecting businesses with project overflow to those with available capacity. Our platform ensures that no opportunity is left on the table, fostering growth for smaller firms and efficiency for larger enterprises. We build bridges between opportunity and capability, driving success across the sector.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by a team of AEC veterans and tech innovators, Bridge Point was born from the recognition of a common industry pain point: mismatched project loads leading to lost revenue and underutilized resources. With over 50 years of combined experience in construction management, engineering, and software development, we've created a secure, user-friendly platform that prioritizes trust and results.

Our Core Values

Credibility

We verify all users through rigorous checks, including business licenses and insurance, to ensure every connection is with a legitimate, reliable partner.

Accountability

Our built-in tools for contracts, milestones, and escrow payments hold all parties accountable, with transparent tracking and dispute resolution processes.

Confidence

Backed by industry-standard security (GDPR/CCPA compliant) and a track record of successful matches, we empower users to engage with peace of mind.
